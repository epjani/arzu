Purchase a Rug that Makes a Difference

By purchasing a handmade ARZU STUDIO HOPE rug, you are helping highly-skilled Afghan woman weavers and their families break the cycle of poverty.

ARZU is an innovative model of social entrepreneurship that provides Afghan women with steady income, access to education and healthcare, and economic empowerment. The purchase of an ARZU rug is an investment in hope.

The Masters Collection is a collaboration between iconic modern architects and rural Afghan weavers. This unique collection elegantly embodies the marriage of high design and traditional techniques.
The Modern + Contemporary Collection features work from top end designers, all exclusive to ARZU. The designs in this collection vary from bold, innovative motifs to subtle, watercolor-inspired patterns.
The Classic Traditional Collection represents our artful weavers' interpretations of floral motifs, highlighting the classic patterns found in Afghanistan and all along the Silk Road. Both Persian and Oriental influences are found in this extensive, diverse and exquisite collection.
The Tribal Collection features designs that have been woven for centuries by tribes in the mountains of central Afghanistan. The fine, supple weave has the look and handle of old Caucasian or Turkmen rugs, predominantly showcasing bold, rich colors and geometric patterns.
